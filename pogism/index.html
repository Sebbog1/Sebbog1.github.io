Pogism

Pogism is a religion that has been passed down through the ages, with a strong community of believers who place a high value on tradition and rituals. The followers of this religion worship a pantheon of deities known as gods, each of whom is believed to have control over a specific aspect of the world.

Theomonty

Theomonty is the God of Time and Space, revered for his control over the flow of time and his ability to move through the vastness of the universe. It is believed that he holds the power to shape the future and influence the past, and that he can be called upon for guidance and protection.

D4

D4 is the God of All Knowledge, believed to have the power to control people's memories and understanding of the world. It is said that he holds the answers to all questions and that he can grant his followers wisdom and understanding.

Makku

Makku is the God of Earth, revered for his control over all aspects of the planet. It is believed that he has the power to shape the land and control the creatures that live on it, and that he can be called upon for protection and guidance.

Rayks

Rayks is the God of Pog, believed to have the power to make anything pog or unpog. He is revered for his ability to bring joy and positivity to the world, and is often called upon for blessings and good fortune.

Valik

Valik is the God of the Underground, revered for his control over the molten depths of the earth. It is believed that he has the power to shape the land and control the creatures that dwell in the underground, and that he can be called upon for guidance and protection.

Sebbog

Sebbog is revered as the one true god of the sea floor, believed to have control over all aspects of the underwater world. It is said that he has the power to shape the oceans and control the creatures that live in them, and that he can be called upon for guidance and protection.

Followers

The followers of Pogism believe in the power of these deities to influence their daily lives and the world around them. They pray to the gods for guidance, protection, and favor, and often make offerings or sacrifices in their honor. Some believers also believe in reincarnation, and that the gods can influence a person's next life. The teachings of Pogism often revolve around living a virtuous and honorable life, in order to gain the favor of the gods and attain a better reincarnation.
