LeafySky

Jesse, also known as LeafySky, was born on April 13, 2006 and is still alive as of today. He is an artist who is skilled in drawing and animation.

The Bad Server

The Bad Server is a Discord server created by Jesse, also known as LeafySky. The server has 12 boosts, which provide role icons and 150 custom emojis. Some of these emojis are memes, while others are drawings created by Jesse himself.

Holidays

During holidays like Christmas or Halloween, Jesse tends to theme his Discord server to match the holiday. This can cause confusion regarding the purpose of each channel.

Family

Jesse has two known family connections: Theosaur, who is his first cousin, and Valerie, who is his second cousin. Family members of Jesse, including Theosaur and Valerie, are given a special role on his Discord server with administrator permissions.
