official ğ thread

The official ğ thread is a thread in The Bad Server that consists of a chain of individuals sending the letter "ğ" or "ğğ" if someone reaches a new level by sending a message.

The Thread

The location of the thread is within the #general chat. The thread's first message was sent by "are" on July 21, 2022 and its contents were "ğ". The creator of the thread is "Novacoot", who was also the second person to send a message in the thread on July 21, 2022.
